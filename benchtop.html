Keithley 179

Keithley 179 is a 4.5 digit 20k count manual ranging multimeter with an optional internal battery pack. These multimeters were designed to be built down to a price whilst holding the Keithely accuracy and design making these a common purchase for the electronics hobbyist. I own a couple of these 192s one with and one without a battery pack and an aditional 20A range.

Keithley 192

Keithely 192 is a 6.5 digit 2M count autoranging DMM from the 1980s capable of measuirng AC Volts, DC Volts and Ohms from micro volts to megaohms with the typical Keithley rock solid accuracy. The 192 is a device I am on the hunt to get at a reasonable price, used or for repair.

Fluke 8000A

Fluke 8000A is a 3.5 digit 2k count manual range multimeter, Fluke is know for its robust electrical engineering equipment and its ability not to kill a user at all costs. Fluke however used to have a toe in the benchtop lab equiptment, despite being only a 2k count meter the Fluke 8000A is precision kit which you can espeically tell from inside the device. After decades this device is still dead on with its results and a venerable device for average-low precision measurements. I ownn a single 8000A and would reccomend this to anyone doing simple measurements for its low price.
